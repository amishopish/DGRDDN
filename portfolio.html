HIMMELSFÄRD (2024)

Bridges and churches are both liminal spaces - a place between a place. A bridge connects us from one side to another, and similarly a church connects heaven with earth. Inspired by the russian montage movement, I wanted to show this connection. Of course the score needed to be of similar magnitude, and I composed this myself using clips from different brass concert instruments. Can we find heaven anywhere or is god just a bridge?

Sommar i Ektorp

A lonely summer, but a peaceful one. WIth not much to do aside from staring at the sky, Ektorp really saved me that year.

I love my cameras audio recording function. I found Ektorp so beautiful and wanted to capture it as memory. With no endproduct in mind I cut together the fragmented videos. And without my knowledge a narrative takes form.- Pieces together of the puzzle beautifully.

STOLPAD (2026)

This is my first ever animation! The inspiration came from a traffic light. The original pictures are painted using acrylic and then later cut out and retouched in GIMP. My brother composed the music, and a little story becomes.
